Who are we?

WorlOfFood is a collection of many hotels and cafes around Kenya who work together to ensure that each person who is hungry and in need of food is served well with food of their choices by our chefs who are well known for their good work in our hotels. We serve you the best in Kenya. We also do donations of food and clothings to our fellow Kenyans who are starving and in much need of help in our country.
